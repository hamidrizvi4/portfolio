Blockchain based serverless To-Do list



Technology used:

Blockchain, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Solidity

started :

6 Nov. 2022

completed :

20 Nov. 2022

A blockchain-based serverless to-do list leverages decentralized technology to ensure secure and tamper-resistant task management. Tasks are recorded on a distributed ledger, enhancing transparency and eliminating the need for a central server. This approach fosters trust and immutability in task tracking, while the serverless architecture ensures scalability and cost-effectiveness. Users can update and access their to-do lists seamlessly, with the blockchain ensuring data integrity and a resilient system.
